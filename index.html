About Us

Here at Jodith Ventures, we develop networks and cooperate with attorneys, mortgage lenders and contractors. interfacing with clients, acting as a go-between for buyers and sellers and performing administrative, research and marketing tasks.


Our Services Includes:

Researching:

To best serve our clients, we must have the pulse of our local market. Here are some of the information we need to collect;



  • Average property prices
  • Market trends
  • Rental rates
  • Vacancy rates
  • Mortgage rates

Valuing Properties:

With all the information we gather from researching our local real estate market, we use that information to value properties. The following goes into valuing propreties;



  • Gather vital information about the property, including the age, size, and other details
  • Perform comparative market analysis to estimate properties’ value
  • Evaluate the condition of the property
  • Understand current market conditions

Marketing:

In addition to marketing our real estate business, JUDITH Ventures is a pro at marketing our client’s properties. Here are a few tasks associated with promoting our client’s properties for sale:



  • Write listing descriptions
  • Develop an email marketing campaign
  • Use social media to advertise a property
  • Use drone photography
  • Film a video tour

Showing Properties:

Here are some of the responsibilities of Jodith Ventures when showing properties



  • Find properties that meet the buyer’s criteria
  • Discuss the pros and cons of each property
  • Accompany potential buyers as they tour the property
  • Help the buyer envision a property’s potential

Hosting Open Houses:

Open houses are a great way to get traffic into our client’s homes.Here are some of the responsibilities of Jodith Ventures when preparing and hosting an open house:



  • Work with the client to ensure the property is ready to be viewed
  • Publicize the open house
  • Interact with potential buyers

Negotiating Transactions:

Whether we are the buyer’s or seller’s agent, part of our job is negotiating for our client’s best interests.Here are some of our responsibilities regarding contract negotiations:



  • Guide clients on market conditions
  • Ensure a fair and honest dealing
  • Negotiate contract terms and price

Drafting Contracts:

Real estate contracts are legal documents. Drafting clear and comprehensive contracts is essential. When drafting a contract, Jodith Ventures meticulously review the following:



  • Agreed-upon terms
  • Contingencies
  • Conditions
  • Deadline
